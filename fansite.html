Meet Shygirl, the DJ, producer and vocalist shaking up the EDM world.

London girl Blane Muise, also known as Shygirl is bringing life back to the club. Inspired by the high-BPM beats that fueled the bedazzeled club culture of the 2000s, Shygirl is an innovator in the techno world.

A producer of critically-acclaimed beats and founder of record label Nuxxe, Muise will be touring around the world throughout the next year, promoting her sixth EP, Clubshy. Will Shygirl be in your city? Find out here.
